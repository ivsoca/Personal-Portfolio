How I Started in This World


First Steps!


My first steps in programming began a couple of years ago when I watched a series called Mr. Robot. From that moment, the world of information technology (IT) captured my interest and sparked a passion I didn't know I had. With a solid foundation, I joined the Ceibal academy in the Jóvenes a Programar program. In this program, I not only learned about programming and web development but also gained practical knowledge in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and Bootstrap. One of the highlights was participating in a group project where we developed an e-commerce site. This experience allowed me to apply what I had learned and work as a team to create a real and functional solution.
